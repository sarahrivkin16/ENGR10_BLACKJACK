BlackJack

Can you beat the house?

What is BlackJack?

BlackJack is a card game with the objective of obtaininga higher total card count than the Dealer by reaching 21 or as close to 21 but not exceeding the count.

Objective:

The objective of BlackJack is to beat the House (aka the Dealer).
To do this you must use your cards to get close to 21 but never over 21.
If you go over 21 GAME OVER and the house wins!!!

Value of Cards:

The cards have the following value:
  1. Aces count as either 1 or 11 depending on the card holder.
  2. Kings, Queens, and Jacks each have a count of 10.
  3. All other cards are counted as their card value.

How to Play:

  1. Place your bet
  2. Two cards are dealt to each player faced up
  3. Evaluate your hand
  4. The Dealer will be revealed at this time
  5. Depending on the total of your cards you may choose:
    • Stand or Stay: Take no additonal cards
    • Hit: Recieve addittional cards
    • Double Down: Double your original bet
    • Split: Add an additional bet to turn 2 cards of the same value into two seperate hands
    • BlackJack: If your first two cards are an ace and a 10-value card you've got a BlackJack
      • You can relax and collect 150% of your original bet (3:2)
  6. If you have the lowest deck you loose your bet
  7. If you were the second to last you have a push
  8. If you won you take the losers bets

Tips:

  1. Start small
  2. Check the dealer's up card
